# 03 - Chapter 01 - Welcome

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Define the engineering, design, and interaction requirements for Chapter One of the Disney Intelligence Platform.

Chapter One is not an onboarding screen.

It is the emotional beginning of the Disney vacation.

Its purpose is to establish trust, reduce planning anxiety, and begin understanding the guest before providing recommendations.

The guest should immediately feel:

"I don't have to figure this out alone."

---

# Chapter Objective

Answer one question before asking any questions.

> "Am I in the right place?"

Only after the guest feels understood should the platform begin learning about the trip.

Understanding always comes before advising.

---

# User Outcome

When the guest completes Chapter One they should feel:

- Heard
- Curious
- Comfortable
- Excited
- Confident enough to continue

The guest should never feel like they completed a form.

---

# Platform Outcome

By the end of Chapter One the platform should understand:

- Why the guest is visiting Disney.
- The emotional goal of the vacation.
- The beginning of the guest profile.
- The tone of future conversations.

Nothing more.

This chapter intentionally gathers very little information.

---

# Opening Experience

The opening should feel quiet.

Warm.

Confident.

Premium.

No dashboards.

No statistics.

No advertisements.

No feature lists.

No mention of artificial intelligence.

No unnecessary animation.

The guest should immediately recognize that this platform is different from traditional planning software.

---

# Opening Message

Planning a Disney vacation comes with a lot to carry.

You don't have to carry it alone.

---

# Primary Question

The conversation begins with one question.

> What are you hoping this trip feels like?

The platform should encourage thoughtful responses.

There are no right or wrong answers.

Examples may include:

- Relaxing
- Magical
- Exciting
- Nostalgic
- Efficient
- Adventurous

The guest may also write their own response.

Free expression should always be supported.

---

# Conversation Standards

The platform should communicate like an experienced Disney friend.

Never like customer support.

Never like enterprise software.

Never like a chatbot.

The guest should forget they are completing onboarding.

Instead, they should feel like someone is genuinely listening.

---

# Interaction Design

The interface should encourage slow, intentional interaction.

Every animation should have purpose.

Every button press should provide subtle haptic feedback.

Transitions should communicate progress rather than speed.

The guest should never feel rushed.

---

# Visual Design

The interface should communicate:

Warmth.

Wonder.

Confidence.

Calm.

Use:

- Warm earth tones.
- Natural lighting.
- Soft gradients.
- Bright magical accents.

Avoid:

- Stark white interfaces.
- Cold corporate colors.
- Visual clutter.
- Excessive text.

---

# Audio Philosophy

If sound is enabled:

Subtle orchestral elements should reinforce important milestones.

Silence should remain the default.

Audio should support emotion rather than demand attention.

---

# Engineering Requirements

Chapter One should:

Load quickly.

Remain responsive.

Support desktop and mobile.

Remember unfinished conversations.

Gracefully recover from interruptions.

Never lose guest responses.

Accessibility should remain a first-class consideration.

---

# Success Criteria

Chapter One succeeds when:

The guest chooses to continue naturally.

The guest feels understood before advice is offered.

The platform gathers meaningful emotional context.

The experience feels more like conversation than software.

---

# Version 1 Scope

Chapter One should only accomplish one objective:

Begin understanding the guest.

Do not introduce:

- Resort recommendations
- Budgets
- Park strategies
- Dining
- Maps
- Reservations
- Operational planning

Those belong in later chapters.

Resist feature creep.

---

# Future Opportunities

Potential future enhancements include:

- Personalized welcome messages.
- Returning guest recognition.
- Adaptive conversation pacing.
- Accessibility personalization.
- Family profile continuity.

These ideas should not delay Version 1.

---

# Final Principle

Chapter One is the emotional handshake between the Disney Intelligence Platform and the guest.

It establishes the relationship that every future recommendation depends upon.

If the guest does not feel understood here, every later feature becomes less valuable.

Trust begins before planning.

Understanding begins before advice.
