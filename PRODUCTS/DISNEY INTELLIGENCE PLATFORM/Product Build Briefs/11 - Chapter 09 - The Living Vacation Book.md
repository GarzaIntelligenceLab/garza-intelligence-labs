# Purpose

Define the engineering, design, and interaction requirements for Chapter Nine of the Disney Intelligence Platform.

Chapter Nine represents the moment the Disney Intelligence Platform transforms from a planning conversation into a living operational companion.

The Living Vacation Book is the guest's personalized operational headquarters before, during, and after their Disney vacation.

It is not the conclusion of planning.

It is the beginning of the Disney experience.

---

# Chapter Objective

Answer one question:

> **"Does this truly feel like our vacation?"**

The Living Vacation Book should demonstrate that the platform listened, understood, and organized everything learned throughout the onboarding journey.

Guests should immediately recognize themselves throughout every page.

---

# User Outcome

By the end of Chapter Nine the guest should feel:

- Excited
- Confident
- Prepared
- Understood
- Curious
- Eager to begin the countdown

The Living Vacation Book should become the first thing guests open every morning and the last thing they review each evening.

---

# Platform Outcome

The platform should generate the first version of the Living Vacation Book.

From this point forward, the Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine assumes responsibility for continuously improving the vacation.

The book is no longer generated once.

It becomes a living operational environment.

---

The Living Vacation Book is not an itinerary.

The platform explains.
