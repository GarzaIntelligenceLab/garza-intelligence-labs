# Purpose

Define the structure, philosophy, and architecture of the Disney Knowledge Graph.

The Disney Knowledge Graph serves as the collective intelligence of the Disney Intelligence Platform.

It organizes every relationship between Disney locations, operations, experiences, historical behavior, and guest interactions into one continuously expanding operational knowledge system.

The Knowledge Graph does not make decisions.

It provides understanding.

---

# Vision

The Disney Intelligence Platform should understand Disney as a living ecosystem.

Not as disconnected attractions.

Not as isolated restaurants.

Not as separate parks.

Everything at Walt Disney World influences something else.

The Knowledge Graph exists to understand those relationships.

---

# Philosophy

Knowledge becomes intelligence only when relationships are understood.

The Disney Knowledge Graph should continuously discover, organize, and strengthen those relationships.

Every new piece of information should make every recommendation smarter.

---

# Mission

Organize.

Connect.

Learn.

Predict.

Support.

The Knowledge Graph exists to provide context for every decision made by the platform.

Connected.
