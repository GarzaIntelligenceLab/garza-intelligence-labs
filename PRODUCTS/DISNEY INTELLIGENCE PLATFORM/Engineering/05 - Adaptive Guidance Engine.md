# 05 - Adaptive Guidance Engine

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Define the Adaptive Guidance Engine that continuously transforms operational intelligence into personalized recommendations.

The Adaptive Guidance Engine is responsible for determining what the guest should know next.

It does not control the vacation.

It continuously identifies the most valuable opportunities available at any given moment.

---

# Vision

Every Disney guest reaches moments of uncertainty.

The Adaptive Guidance Engine exists to remove that uncertainty.

Rather than forcing a single path, the engine continuously evaluates the vacation and presents the guest with the best available opportunities based on current conditions and everything the platform has learned.

The goal is not perfect planning.

The goal is confident decision making.

---

# Philosophy

The future changes.

Weather changes.

Wait times change.

Transportation changes.

People change.

The vacation should change with them.

Static itineraries become outdated.

Adaptive guidance keeps the vacation alive.

---

# Mission

Observe.

Evaluate.

Recommend.

Explain.

Repeat.

The engine should quietly operate throughout the vacation without demanding constant attention.

---

# Core Principles

Every recommendation should be:

Relevant.

Timely.

Explainable.

Personalized.

Optional.

Confidence-driven.

Operationally beneficial.

---

# Recommendation Philosophy

The engine should never tell guests what they must do.

Instead, it should answer:

> "Given everything happening right now, what are your best opportunities?"

Recommendations should empower.

Never control.

# Adaptive Paths

Whenever meaningful change occurs, the engine should generate multiple intelligent paths.

Example:

## Current Situation

Heavy rain expected in 45 minutes.

Guest ahead of schedule.

Dinner reservation remains unchanged.

---

### Path One

Thrill Window

Prioritize nearby attractions before weather arrives.

---

### Path Two

Food Adventure

Begin World Showcase dining before crowds increase.

---

### Path Three

Slow Magic

Explore quieter locations before dinner.

Each path should explain:

Why it exists.

Who it benefits.

What it prioritizes.

What it sacrifices.

# Engineering Requirements

The Adaptive Guidance Engine should:

Continuously evaluate operational conditions.

Generate recommendations in real time.

Support graceful degradation when live data becomes unavailable.

Explain every recommendation.

Integrate with the Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine.

Support future predictive intelligence.

Operate with minimal latency.

Recommendations arrive at meaningful moments.

The platform quietly improves the vacation throughout the day.
