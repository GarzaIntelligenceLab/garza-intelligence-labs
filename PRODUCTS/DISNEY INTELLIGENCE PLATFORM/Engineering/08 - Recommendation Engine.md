# 08 - Recommendation Engine

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Define the Recommendation Engine responsible for generating personalized recommendations throughout the Disney Intelligence Platform.

The Recommendation Engine transforms intelligence into meaningful recommendations that align with the guest's goals, preferences, operational conditions, and current vacation state.

Recommendations should never be generic.

Every recommendation should feel intentional.

---

# Vision

No two Disney vacations should receive identical recommendations.

Every recommendation should reflect:

- The guest.
- The vacation.
- The current moment.

The Recommendation Engine should create recommendations that feel obvious after reading them, but impossible before receiving them.

---

# Philosophy

Recommendations are earned.

The platform should never recommend something simply because it is popular.

Popularity is one input.

Compatibility is the objective.

---

# Mission

Understand.

Evaluate.

Rank.

Explain.

Deliver.

Repeat.

---

# Core Principles

Every recommendation should be:

Personalized.

Relevant.

Explainable.

Actionable.

Adaptive.

Confidence-driven.

---

# Recommendation Inputs

Recommendations should consider:

## Guest Intelligence Profile

- Vacation philosophy
- Travel style
- Budget philosophy
- Energy profile
- Disney experience
- Accessibility
- Dining preferences
- Attraction priorities

---

## Disney Knowledge Graph

- Attraction relationships
- Dining relationships
- Transportation
- Historical intelligence
- Walking paths
- Crowd behavior

---

## Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine

- Live wait times
- Weather
- Transportation
- Reservations
- Crowd movement
- Ride closures
- Current operational state

---

# Recommendation Categories

The engine should generate recommendations for:

- Attractions
- Dining
- Entertainment
- Transportation
- Resorts
- Shopping
- Rest
- Hidden Gems
- Seasonal Experiences
- Operational Opportunities

---

# Recommendation Scoring

Every recommendation should receive a continuously calculated score based upon:

Guest Compatibility

Operational Benefit

Walking Efficiency

Time Sensitivity

Budget Alignment

Historical Success

Current Conditions

Recommendation Confidence

The highest score should not automatically become the recommendation.

Human experience always outweighs mathematical optimization.

---

# Recommendation Lifecycle

Every recommendation follows the same lifecycle.

Observe.

Generate.

Rank.

Explain.

Present.

Learn.

Improve.

---

# Recommendation Transparency

Every recommendation should explain:

Why it was selected.

Why it matters.

What tradeoffs exist.

Why now is the appropriate time.

Guests should understand recommendations rather than blindly trust them.

---

# Recommendation Expiration

Recommendations should expire naturally.

Outdated recommendations should disappear without creating confusion.

The platform should always reflect current reality.

---

# Learning Loop

The engine should continuously learn from:

Accepted recommendations.

Ignored recommendations.

Rejected recommendations.

Vacation outcomes.

Guest satisfaction.

Every recommendation improves future recommendations.

---

# Engineering Requirements

The Recommendation Engine should:

Operate continuously.

Support real-time updates.

Generate explainable recommendations.

Integrate with every intelligence system.

Scale as new recommendation categories are introduced.

---

# Success Criteria

The Recommendation Engine succeeds when:

Guests consistently feel recommendations are helpful.

Recommendations feel personalized.

Trust increases over time.

Guests naturally rely upon the platform.

---

# Future Opportunities

Future versions may include:

Predictive recommendations.

Cross-family learning.

AI reasoning summaries.

Collaborative recommendations.

Predictive vacation optimization.

---

# Final Principle

Recommendations should never feel like advertisements.

They should feel like thoughtful advice from someone who genuinely understands both Disney and the guest.

---

## Notes

The Recommendation Engine transforms operational intelligence into meaningful opportunities that improve every Disney vacation.
