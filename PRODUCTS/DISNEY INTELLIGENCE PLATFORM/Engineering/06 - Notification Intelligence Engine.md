# 06 - Notification Intelligence Engine

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Define the Notification Intelligence Engine responsible for determining when, why, and how the Disney Intelligence Platform communicates with guests.

Notifications are not reminders.

Notifications are operational intelligence delivered at the right moment.

Every notification should create measurable value.

If a notification does not improve the guest's vacation, it should not be sent.

---

# Vision

The Disney Intelligence Platform should never compete for the guest's attention.

It should earn it.

Notifications should feel like a knowledgeable friend quietly tapping the guest on the shoulder only when something meaningful has happened.

The best notification is one that helps.

The second best notification is no notification at all.

---

# Philosophy

Attention is one of the guest's most valuable resources.

Every interruption carries a cost.

Notifications should reduce cognitive load.

Never increase it.

The platform should communicate with intention.

Not frequency.

---

# Mission

Observe.

Evaluate.

Determine value.

Communicate only when beneficial.

Return to silence.

---

# Core Principles

Every notification should be:

Relevant.

Timely.

Personalized.

Explainable.

Actionable.

Respectful.

Optional.

---

# Notification Philosophy

The platform should never notify guests simply because new information exists.

It should notify guests when new information changes the quality of a decision.

Information alone is not valuable.

Actionable intelligence is.

# Notification Evaluation

Before sending any notification, the engine should ask:

Has something meaningful changed?

Does this benefit the guest?

Is this the right moment?

Would the guest expect this?

Does the value exceed the interruption?

If the answer is no...

Remain silent.

---

# Priority Levels

## Critical

Requires immediate attention.

Examples:

Ride closure affecting reservation.

Transportation disruption.

Weather emergency.

Reservation issue.

---

## High

Creates meaningful operational improvement.

Examples:

Significantly lower wait times.

Dining opportunity.

Earlier transportation.

Unexpected operational advantage.

---

## Medium

Improves planning.

Examples:

Packing reminder.

Countdown milestone.

Reservation reminder.

Park opening reminder.

---

## Low

Interesting but nonessential.

Examples:

Disney trivia.

Historical facts.

Planning inspiration.

Vacation memories.

Low-priority notifications should never interrupt the guest.

---

# Notification Timing

The engine should consider:

Current activity.

Current park.

Walking.

Dining.

Entertainment.

Sleep hours.

Driving.

Travel days.

Guest engagement.

Timing should feel natural.

Never disruptive.

---

# Notification Content

Every notification should answer three questions:

What happened?

Why does it matter?

What should I do?

Guests should never open a notification confused.

---

# Adaptive Intelligence

Notification frequency should adapt.

Guests who engage frequently may receive additional opportunities.

Guests who ignore notifications should receive fewer.

The platform should continuously learn the guest's preferred communication style.

---

# Learning Loop

The engine should learn from:

Opened notifications.

Ignored notifications.

Dismissed notifications.

Accepted recommendations.

Vacation outcomes.

Guest feedback.

The objective is not to send more notifications.

The objective is to send better notifications.

---

# Engineering Requirements

The Notification Intelligence Engine should:

Support real-time delivery.

Support scheduled delivery.

Support quiet hours.

Support location awareness.

Support vacation phase awareness.

Prevent duplicate notifications.

Prioritize meaningful communication.

Synchronize across devices.

Operate even during temporary network interruptions.

---

# Success Criteria

The Notification Intelligence Engine succeeds when:

Guests rarely disable notifications.

Notifications consistently improve decisions.

Guests trust the platform's timing.

Every notification feels worthwhile.
