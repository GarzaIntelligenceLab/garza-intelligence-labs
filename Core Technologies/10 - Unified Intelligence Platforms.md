# Purpose

Define the Unified Intelligence Platform (UIP), the primary product architecture developed by Garza Intelligence Labs.

A Unified Intelligence Platform transforms fragmented information, disconnected operations, and complex human systems into one continuously learning operational environment.

Every Garza Intelligence Labs product is a Unified Intelligence Platform.

---

# Definition

A Unified Intelligence Platform is an integrated operational intelligence system built upon the core technologies of Garza Intelligence Labs.

Rather than functioning as a collection of disconnected tools, a Unified Intelligence Platform continuously organizes information, understands relationships, reasons through changing conditions, and provides intelligent guidance that improves human capability.

Every platform is designed around a specific Complex Human System.
