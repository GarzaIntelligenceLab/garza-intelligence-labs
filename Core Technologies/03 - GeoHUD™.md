# Purpose

Define GeoHUD™, the spatial intelligence interface developed by Garza Intelligence Labs.

GeoHUD™ continuously delivers contextual operational intelligence based upon a person's physical location, current environment, objectives, and changing operational conditions.

It serves as the primary interface between people and Complex Human Systems.

---

# Definition

GeoHUD™ is a location-aware operational intelligence interface that continuously presents the right information, guidance, and recommendations at the right place and the right time.

Unlike traditional maps or navigation systems, GeoHUD™ does not simply answer where a person is.

It answers:

Where they are.

What matters.

What has changed.

Where they should go.

Why they should go there.

What will likely happen next.

---

GeoHUD™ transforms physical spaces into understandable operational environments.
