# Purpose

Develop a comprehensive understanding of Disney guest psychology before, during, and after a Walt Disney World vacation.

The Disney Intelligence Platform exists to improve the guest experience.

To improve the experience, Garza Intelligence Labs must first understand the people experiencing it.

This document serves as the foundational psychological research for the Disney Knowledge Graph.

---

# Research Objective

Understand how Disney guests think, feel, behave, and make decisions throughout every stage of their vacation.

Research should focus on identifying emotional patterns, behavioral trends, operational pain points, and opportunities where intelligent guidance can improve the overall experience.

---

# Operational Importance

Guest psychology influences every major system within the Disney Intelligence Platform.

Examples include:

- Adaptive Guidance
- GeoHUD™
- Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine
- Recommendation Engine
- Notification Intelligence Engine
- Living Vacation Book

Understanding people improves every recommendation.
