# 07 - Intelligence Augmentation

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Define how Operational Intelligence Navigators use Intelligence Augmentation to increase research efficiency while preserving intellectual integrity.

Operational Intelligence is created by people.

Artificial intelligence is a tool that assists the research process.

It does not replace investigation, analysis, or judgment.

---

# Philosophy

Garza Intelligence Labs does not use artificial intelligence to think for Operational Intelligence Navigators.

Artificial intelligence exists to reduce repetitive work, reveal patterns, organize information, and assist discovery.

The responsibility for every conclusion always belongs to the Navigator.

Thinking cannot be delegated.

---

# Mission

The purpose of Intelligence Augmentation is to allow Operational Intelligence Navigators to spend more time thinking and less time performing repetitive tasks.

Every use of artificial intelligence should increase human capability.

Never replace it.

---

# Appropriate Uses

Artificial intelligence is encouraged for:

Research planning.

Brainstorming additional research questions.

Identifying recurring patterns.

Finding contradictions between sources.

Creating timelines.

Comparing multiple articles.

Identifying missing information.

Generating Knowledge Graph relationship suggestions.

Organizing notes.

Searching large collections of information.

Exploring alternative perspectives.

Generating follow-up questions.

Identifying operational friction.

Reviewing work for clarity and consistency.

Artificial intelligence should accelerate discovery.

---

# Inappropriate Uses

Operational Intelligence Navigators should never rely on artificial intelligence to produce work requiring independent reasoning.

Examples include:

Writing Intelligence Reports.

Writing literature reviews.

Writing executive summaries.

Drawing research conclusions.

Creating final recommendations.

Determining source credibility.

Making operational judgments.

Writing final mission deliverables.

Operational Intelligence requires human thought.

These responsibilities belong to the Navigator.

---

# Human Responsibility

Artificial intelligence may suggest.

Artificial intelligence may organize.

Artificial intelligence may compare.

Artificial intelligence may accelerate.

Artificial intelligence may never replace professional judgment.

Every report submitted to Garza Intelligence Labs represents the work of the Operational Intelligence Navigator responsible for the mission.

---

# Verification

Every AI-assisted finding should be independently verified.

Navigators are responsible for:

Reviewing original sources.

Checking every citation.

Identifying hallucinations.

Confirming relationships.

Evaluating confidence.

Protecting the integrity of the Living Knowledge Graph.

Verification is mandatory.

---

# Prompt Library

Garza Intelligence Labs maintains an official Prompt Library for Operational Intelligence Navigators.

Prompt categories include:

Research Planning.

Pattern Recognition.

Contradiction Analysis.

Knowledge Graph Relationships.

Research Question Generation.

Operational Friction Discovery.

Missing Research Detection.

Source Cross-Comparison.

Prompt standardization improves consistency across the Research Division.

---

# Standard

Artificial intelligence should remove repetitive work.

Operational Intelligence Navigators should spend their time:

Thinking.

Questioning.

Analyzing.

Discovering.

Connecting ideas.

Generating original insight.

The value of Garza Intelligence Labs comes from human reasoning supported by intelligent tools.

---

# Final Principle

Artificial intelligence accelerates research.

Operational Intelligence Navigators create intelligence.

Those responsibilities should never be confused.
