# 05 - Disney Research Master Roadmap

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Establish the complete research roadmap required to build and continuously improve the Disney Intelligence Platform.

This document serves as the master index for every Disney research initiative.

Research should not be performed randomly.

Every project should strengthen the Disney Knowledge Graph and improve the Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine.

---

# Philosophy

Disney is not a collection of attractions.

Disney is a living operational ecosystem.

Every component influences another.

Understanding these relationships is more valuable than understanding isolated facts.

---

# Mission

Develop the world's most complete operational understanding of Walt Disney World.

Research should improve:

Guest confidence.

Operational intelligence.

Recommendation quality.

Adaptive guidance.

Future product development.

---

# Research Domains

Research every park as a complete operational system.

Topics include:

- Layout
- Attractions
- Dining
- Entertainment
- Crowd flow
- Walking networks
- Hidden experiences
- Seasonal operations

---

Research should generally follow this sequence:

1. Guest Psychology

2. Transportation

3. Resorts

4. Parks

5. Attractions

6. Dining

Verified findings.

Operational implications.

Unknown questions.

Product opportunities.

Future research recommendations.

Knowledge Graph updates.

---

# Success Criteria

Research succeeds when:

The Disney Knowledge Graph becomes more intelligent.

Recommendations improve.

Operational confidence increases.

Guests receive better guidance.

---

# Final Principle

The objective is not to know everything about Disney.

The objective is to understand Disney well enough to continuously improve every guest's vacation.

Knowledge without application has little value.

Operational intelligence changes experiences.
