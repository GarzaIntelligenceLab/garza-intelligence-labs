# Mission Intent

The purpose of this Intelligence Mission is to establish the behavioral foundation of the Disney Living Knowledge Graph.

Every recommendation generated by Garza Intelligence Labs ultimately influences a human decision.

Before GIL can intelligently guide guests, it must first understand how guests think, feel, behave, and make decisions throughout a Disney vacation.

Upon successful completion, this mission should strengthen future Recommendation Engines, Adaptive Guidance, GeoHUD™, Conversation Engines, and future Unified Intelligence Platforms.

---

# Mission Background

Disney vacations represent one of the most emotionally significant consumer experiences in the world.

Guests invest substantial amounts of:

• Time

• Money

• Emotional energy

• Family expectations

• Planning effort

Unlike routine travel, Disney vacations frequently begin months before arrival and continue influencing behavior long after the vacation concludes.

Understanding this psychological journey represents one of the foundational responsibilities of Garza Intelligence Labs.

Traditional Disney planning resources primarily answer logistical questions.

Garza Intelligence Labs seeks to answer behavioral questions.
