## Welcome to Garza Intelligence Labs

You are joining an organization founded on a simple belief:
Every organization deserves the opportunity to reach its full capability.
Our purpose is not simply to build software.
We exist to transform complexity into clarity by organizing information, strengthening operations, and continuously optimizing the way organizations work.
At Garza Intelligence Labs, we believe technology should never become the center of attention.
People should.
Technology is one of many tools we use to help individuals and organizations spend less time managing unnecessary complexity and more time creating meaningful impact.
Everything you build here should reflect our core beliefs.
Every decision should align with the Triple O Philosophy.
Every product should reduce friction.
Every interaction should leave people more confident than before.
We value curiosity over ego.
Learning over assumptions.
Progress over perfection.
If you improve something today, even by one percent, you have helped move Garza Intelligence Labs forward.
Welcome to the team.
Now let's build the future together.

Austin Garza
Founder
Garza Intelligence Labs
