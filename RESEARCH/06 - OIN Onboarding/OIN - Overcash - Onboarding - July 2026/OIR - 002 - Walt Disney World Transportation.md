# Mission Intent

The purpose of this Intelligence Mission is to establish a foundational understanding of Walt Disney World's transportation infrastructure and its influence on guest movement, operational efficiency, and decision making.

Transportation represents more than moving guests between locations.

It influences arrival confidence, park strategy, dining decisions, emotional state, resort selection, and overall vacation satisfaction.

This mission will establish transportation as a core operational system within the Disney Living Knowledge Graph and provide foundational intelligence for future Recommendation Engines, GeoHUD™, Adaptive Guidance, and the Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine (DOIE).

---

# Mission Background

Walt Disney World operates one of the most complex guest transportation systems in the hospitality industry.

Transportation includes:

• Monorails

• Disney Bus Transportation

• Disney Skyliner

• Watercraft

• Walking Paths

• Minnie Van Service

• Personal Vehicles

• Ride Share Services

Each transportation system offers unique operational advantages, limitations, and behavioral effects.

Traditional travel resources often compare transportation using travel time alone.

Garza Intelligence Labs seeks to understand transportation as a dynamic operational network influenced by infrastructure, guest behavior, environmental conditions, operational variability, and system reliability.
