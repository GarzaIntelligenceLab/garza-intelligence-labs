# 03 - How Research Flows Through GIL

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Purpose

Define how research moves through Garza Intelligence Labs.

Every research project contributes to a larger operational intelligence pipeline.

Understanding this pipeline is essential for every Operational Intelligence Navigator.

No research exists in isolation.

Every contribution should strengthen the organization.

---

# The Philosophy

Research is not the final product.

Research is the beginning.

Its purpose is to improve Operational Intelligence.

Operational Intelligence improves our technologies.

Our technologies improve our products.

Our products expand human capability.

Every research project should contribute to that mission.

---

# The Intelligence Pipeline

Every Intelligence Mission follows the same operational process.

```
Question

↓

Research

↓

Evidence

↓

Analysis

↓

Intelligence Report

↓

Living Knowledge Graph

↓

Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine

↓

Operational Intelligence

↓

Products

↓

Human Capability
```

Research is valuable because it strengthens every stage that follows.

---

# Step One

## Ask Better Questions

Every Intelligence Mission begins with a question worth answering.

Examples include:

- Why do Disney guests become overwhelmed?

- What causes operational friction?

- How do transportation systems influence guest satisfaction?

- Which experiences consistently exceed expectations?

Good intelligence begins with meaningful questions.

---

# Step Two

## Conduct Research

Operational Intelligence Navigators investigate the question using approved research methods.

Research should be:

Evidence-based.

Well documented.

Objective.

Repeatable.

Supported by reliable sources.

Research should never begin with a conclusion.

---

# Step Three

## Analyze Findings

Information alone has limited value.

Navigators should identify:

Patterns.

Relationships.

Operational significance.

Unexpected findings.

Future questions.

The objective is understanding—not information collection.

---

# Step Four

## Produce Intelligence

Research becomes operational intelligence when conclusions are supported by evidence and clearly documented.

Every Intelligence Report should answer:

What was learned?

Why does it matter?

How confident are we?

What should change?

What new questions were discovered?

---

# Step Five

## Strengthen the Living Knowledge Graph

Verified intelligence becomes part of the Living Knowledge Graph.

The Living Knowledge Graph represents the organization's continuously expanding understanding of Complex Human Systems.

Every verified contribution strengthens future research and future products.

---

# Step Six

## Improve the Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine

The Dynamic Operational Intelligence Engine reasons using the intelligence stored within the Living Knowledge Graph.

As the Knowledge Graph improves:

Recommendations improve.

Adaptive Guidance improves.

Operational Intelligence improves.

Products improve.

Research directly strengthens the intelligence of the organization.

---

# Step Seven

## Improve Products

Operational Intelligence influences every Unified Intelligence Platform developed by Garza Intelligence Labs.

Research may improve:

Recommendations.

GeoHUD™.

Conversation Engine.

Adaptive Guidance.

Future products.

Engineering priorities.

No research should end without considering its impact.

---

# Step Eight

## Expand Human Capability

The final objective of every Intelligence Mission is not completing a report.

It is expanding human capability.

Every improvement should help people:

Understand more.

Stress less.

Decide better.

Navigate complexity with confidence.

This is the ultimate purpose of Operational Intelligence.

---

# Your Role

As an Operational Intelligence Navigator, you are responsible for the first and most important stage of this pipeline.

Without disciplined research, Operational Intelligence cannot improve.

Without Operational Intelligence, our technologies cannot evolve.

Without better technologies, human capability cannot expand.

Every Intelligence Mission matters.

---

# Final Principle

Research does not stop when a report is finished.

Research continues through every recommendation, every product improvement, and every person whose experience becomes better because understanding increased.

Operational Intelligence begins with you.
