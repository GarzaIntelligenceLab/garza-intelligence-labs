## Purpose

Define the key terms and words that are excluse to Garza Intelligence Labs, without these terms GIL itself will only be an idea. 

## GIL Glossary 
### Intelligence:
- The capability to understand, infer, analyze, organize, and present information in a way that improves human understanding and decision-making. 
### Capability:
- The full potential of an individual, organization, or system realized through clarity, disciplined operations, continuous improvement, and the ability to overcome constraints.
### Triple O Philosophy:
- The operational philosophy of Garza Intelligence Labs that transforms organizations through Organization, Operations, and Optimization to unlock greater capability.
### Unified Intelligence Platform (UIP):
- A software platform that transforms fragmented information into organized, actionable operational intelligence. 
### Clarity:
- The absence of unnecessary complexity, allowing information to be understood and acted upon with confidence.
### Operational Intelligence:
- The practical application of organized information to improve decisions, workflows, and organizational capability.
