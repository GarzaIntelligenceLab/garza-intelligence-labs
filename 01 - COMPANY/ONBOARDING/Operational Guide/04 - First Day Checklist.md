# 04 - First Day Checklist

> **Status:** Approved
>
> **Version:** 1.0
>
> **Last Updated:** July 2026

---

## Purpose

Provide every Garza Intelligence Labs team member with a consistent first-day experience that establishes the philosophy, operational standards, and development environment of the organization.

The goal is not simply to complete setup.

The goal is to become operational.

---

## Before You Begin

Complete the following Operational Guides:

- 01 - Required Software.md
- 02 - Development Environment.md
- 03 - GitHub Standards.md

---

## Phase I — Setup

Complete the following:

□ Install all required software.

□ Sign into GitHub.

□ Sign into Visual Studio Code.

□ Verify Git integration.

□ Verify Markdown functionality.

□ Verify access to the GIL workspace.

---

## Phase II — Learn the Canon

Read the complete GIL Canon.

The purpose is not memorization.

The purpose is understanding.

Complete:

□ Founder's Letter

□ Mission

□ Vision

□ Core Philosophies

□ Triple O Philosophy

□ Product Philosophy

□ Decision Principles

□ Capability Framework

□ GIL Glossary

Review any active product doctrine relevant to your role.

---

## Phase III — Understand the Business

By the end of your first day, you should understand:

- Why Garza Intelligence Labs exists.
- The Triple O Philosophy.
- The Capability Framework.
- Why technology is a tool rather than the mission.
- Why Operational Intelligence is the company's primary discipline.
- The current Version 1 priorities.

---

## Phase IV — Meet the Team

Meet with executive leadership.

Discuss:

- Company mission
- Current objectives
- Active priorities
- Questions regarding the Canon
- Questions regarding operational standards

Understanding is encouraged.

Assumption is discouraged.

---

## Phase V — Begin Contributing

After completing onboarding:

□ Verify assigned repositories.

□ Review current priorities.

□ Select an approved task.

□ Begin contributing according to established GIL standards.

---

## First Day Success

Your first day is successful when:

- Your workstation is operational.
- You understand the philosophy of Garza Intelligence Labs.
- You understand the current priorities.
- You know where to find documentation.
- You are prepared to contribute with confidence.

---

## Final Reminder

Garza Intelligence Labs is built upon continuous learning.

Questions are encouraged.

Curiosity is expected.

Improvement is continuous.

Protect the philosophy.

Strengthen the organization.

Leave every system better than you found it.
