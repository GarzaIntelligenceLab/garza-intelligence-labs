# Garza Intelligence Labs

> **Expanding Human Capability Through Operational Intelligence**

---

## Overview

Garza Intelligence Labs (GIL) is an Operational Intelligence company dedicated to understanding, improving, and navigating Complex Human Systems.

Rather than building isolated software applications, GIL develops reusable technologies that transform complexity into clarity, clarity into confidence, and confidence into human capability.

Our mission is to help people spend less time managing complexity and more time focused on work, relationships, creativity, learning, and experiences that matter most.

---

# Mission

Garza Intelligence Labs exists to study, understand, and improve Complex Human Systems through Operational Intelligence.

We believe that increasing complexity should not reduce human capability.

Instead, intelligent systems should organize information, reason through changing environments, and provide contextual guidance that allows people to make better decisions with greater confidence.

---

# Vision

To become the global leader in Operational Intelligence by developing technologies that help people navigate increasingly complex environments across every major industry.

Disney is our first proving ground.

It will not be our last.

# What We Study

Garza Intelligence Labs studies **Complex Human Systems**.

A Complex Human System is any environment where people, information, physical spaces, operations, technology, and time continuously interact to produce dynamic outcomes.

Examples include:

- Entertainment
- Healthcare
- Education
- Transportation
- Manufacturing
- Finance
- Government
- Hospitality
- Logistics
- Smart Cities

Every industry contains Complex Human Systems.

Every Complex Human System can benefit from Operational Intelligence.

# The Triple O Philosophy

Every technology developed by Garza Intelligence Labs follows the Triple O Philosophy.

**Organization**

Transform fragmented information into structured understanding.

**Operations**

Understand how systems function, evolve, and influence people.

**Optimization**

Continuously improve systems through Operational Intelligence.

Triple O is the foundational methodology that guides every research initiative, engineering decision, and product developed by Garza Intelligence Labs.

# Current Product

## Disney Intelligence Platform (DIP)

The Disney Intelligence Platform is the first Unified Intelligence Platform developed by Garza Intelligence Labs.

DIP applies Operational Intelligence to one of the world's most operationally complex entertainment environments.

Its objective is to reduce planning stress, improve decision making, and continuously adapt throughout an entire Disney vacation.

The Disney Intelligence Platform serves as the proving ground for many of the technologies being developed by Garza Intelligence Labs.
