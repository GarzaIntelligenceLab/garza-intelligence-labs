## Purpose

Define the decision-making principles that guide the culture, products, and long-term direction of Garza Intelligence Labs.
---

## The GIL Decision Principles

### 1. Clarity Above Everything
If a feature makes the product more confusing than useful, it does not belong.

### 2. Time Is the Most Valuable Resource
Every product should give people time back.
If it saves clicks but wastes thinking, it failed.

### 3. Organization Creates Confidence
When information is organized, people make better decisions.

### 4. Simplicity Is Not the Same as Basic
Simple products can solve incredibly complex problems. 

### 5. Technology Is Never the Goal
Technology is only valuable if it solves a real problem.
We build solutions—not technology for technology's sake.

### 6. We Question Every Standard
We respect existing ideas, but we are not obligated to copy them.
If a better solution exists, we build it.

### 7. Every Product Must Pass Triple O
- Organization
- Operations
- Optimization
If it doesn't improve at least one...
It doesn't ship.

### 8. AI Is a Tool, Never the Mission
Artificial intelligence should amplify human capability—not replace human judgment.

### 9. Confidence Is Success
When customers finish using our products, they should feel more confident than when they started.

### 10. Make Complexity Manageable
Everything we build should reduce cognitive burden and operational friction.
